COMPANY PROFILE

Automobile Lamborghini was founded in 1963, is headquartered in SantAgata Bolognese, in Northern Italy. The Lamborghini Urus, its third model line and the first Super SUV, was launched in 2017, creating a new niche in the luxury segment with benchmark power, performance and driving dynamics, unparalleled design, luxury and daily usability. Over half a century Automobile Lamborghini created a continuous series of dream cars, including the 350 GT, Miura, Espada, Countach, Diablo, as well as limited editions such as the Reventon, Sesto Elemento, Veneno and the Centenario. With the Lamborghini Sian FKP 37 unveiled in 2019, a few-off build of only 63 units, the company delivers unique new hybrid technologies, featuring the world-first application of a supercapacitor for hybridization, new materials technology, and unsurpassed Lamborghini performance.
